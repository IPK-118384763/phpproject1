England

I visited England back in 2013 with my Family . We stayed in London for most of the trip.

London was unlink any city I had visited before it was

bustling with people and it was sometimes quite difficult to navigate around.

We did most of the famous tourist attractions of London like riding the

London eye, Seeing Big Ben and Buckingham Palace. I found that travelling by the tube was the easiest way to get around.

Portugal

I visited the Algarve region of Portugal. I stayed in the coastal city of Albufeira.

Albufeira was a beautiful city. I was very lucky because the hotel I stayed in was close to both the old and new town of Albufeira.

The hotel was not only close to the town but also had it's own pool for the guests.

Albufeira also had beautiful beaches that I often visited.

France

I visited France in 2015. I stayed in Paris in a cozy apartment.

I found a new love for coffee as there was many quaint cafes scattered around the city.

I saw many of the usual tourist attractions such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre but my favourite attraction was mount martre.

The architecture of the city was amazing

Spain

I visited Spain in 2017. I stayed in Barcelona in a holiday home near the sea.

I tried loads of local food and I loved the dish called esqueixada de bacallà which is shredded salted cod on bed of tomatoes,

red peppers, onions and olives with an olive oil dressing. The city was really busy but everything is really close. I visited

La Sagrada Familia temple, The Font Mágica Fountain, Picasso Museum, and the Las Ramblas street

Scotland

I visited Scotland in 2019. I stayed in Edinburgh in a nice hotel in the old town of Edinburgh.

I visited many tourist attractions but my favourite was Edinburgh a Castle, I loved learning the history

of the castle. Walking was the easiest way to get around and it was very easy to navigate around the city.

The city was very beautiful and the people were very nice and friendly
